Our Heritage

Five Centuries of Healing

From a humble clinic in Ming Dynasty China to Orchard Road, Singapore. The Yuan Tai Tang tradition endures.

In the fifteenth year of Emperor Zhengde's reign (1520), in the ancient town of Zhouzhuang, a practitioner named Yuan Jiaji established a humble clinic. He called it "Yuan Tai Tang" — Yuan's Hall of Great Peace.

Yuan Jiaji treated the poor without payment and never turned a patient away.

Under the reign of Emperor Qianlong, the clinic's reputation flourished. Through five centuries, the Yuan family kept refining their methods over generations.

Now, Yuan Jinsheng, the latest inheritor, has brought Yuan Tai Tang to Singapore. The methods haven't changed, but the clinic has.
